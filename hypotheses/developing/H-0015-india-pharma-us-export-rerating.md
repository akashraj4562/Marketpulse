## Statement
**Cause:** US drug pricing reform (IRA Inflation Reduction Act Medicare drug negotiation) and accelerated FDA generic approvals are creating a bifurcated pharma landscape. Companies with complex injectable/specialty generics pipelines (Sun Pharma, Dr. Reddy's, Zydus Lifesciences) are gaining US market share as brand-name drugs face price pressure and branded players accelerate generic settlements. Meanwhile, the FDA has cleared import alerts at several Indian facilities over 2024–2026, unblocking previously delayed approvals.

**Effect:** Sun Pharma, Dr. Reddy's, and Zydus see meaningful US revenue acceleration in FY27, driving earnings upgrades and P/E re-rating toward historical highs.

**One-line:** FDA import alert resolutions + IRA brand price pressure → Indian specialty generic opportunity → Sun Pharma / Dr. Reddy's US revenue +15–25% FY27 → P/E rerating to 25–30x.
