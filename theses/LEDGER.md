## How to read this ledger

- **Confidence:** H = High (>70%), M = Medium (40–70%), L = Low (20–40%), Spec = Speculative (<20%)
- **Status:** Live (active, unresolved) · Confirmed (chain played out as predicted) · Falsified (chain broke) · Expired (timeframe passed, outcome unclear) · Unsupported (red-team rejected; not filed as active thesis)
- **Outcome:** filled in when the thesis resolves — what actually happened, and what the chain got right/wrong
